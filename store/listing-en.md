## Full description

```
Prepare for the Polish Civil Aviation Authority (ULC) theoretical exam for private pilot licences, with questions based on the ULC question bank.

Supported licences:
• PPL(A) - aeroplane
• PPL(H) - helicopter
• SPL - sailplane
• BPL - balloon

Inside the app:
• Untimed learning mode with immediate feedback on each answer
• Exam simulation with countdown timer
• All nine subjects: Air Law, Meteorology, Navigation, Principles of Flight, Aircraft General Knowledge, Flight Planning, Human Performance & Limitations, Operational Procedures, Communications
• Favourites list for quick review
• Light and dark theme
• Works offline after the first content download
• No ads, no account, no tracking

The app is free and non-commercial.
```
